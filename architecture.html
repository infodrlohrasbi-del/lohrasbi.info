Three-Layer System Framework

1. Strategic Architecture Layer

The structural and governance backbone of the National Health OS — defining macro–micro alignment, resilience logic, policy kernels, crisis envelopes, and national operating principles. These modules form the constitutional logic of a health nation.

2. AI Cognitive Layer

The intelligence substrate governing predictive reasoning, multi-agent orchestration, NLP interoperability, reinforcement models, and autonomous decision loops. This layer upgrades a health system from static to adaptive.

3. Urban, Logistic & Infrastructure Layer

The physical operating environment — mobility networks, diagnostic load balancing, national supply-chain orchestration, telehealth security, and capacity marketplaces. These modules anchor clinical operations into real-world flow.
