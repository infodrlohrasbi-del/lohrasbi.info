Dr. Lohrasbi works at the intersection of healthcare transformation, AI strategy, and systems architecture. His work operationalizes Symbiotic Intelligence – integrating human judgment, governance, and AI engines across hospitals, networks, and national health systems.

He designs crisis-ready operating models, Health Nation OS frameworks, data and intelligence pipelines, and architectural blueprints that strengthen resilience, reduce failure modes, and build antifragility by design.
