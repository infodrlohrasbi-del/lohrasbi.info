Executive Summary

Healthcare systems, AI strategy, and antifragile architecture.

Designing crisis-ready, AI-augmented health systems that integrate governance, operations, and intelligence across hospitals, networks, and national health platforms.

Dr. Nader Lohrasbi works at the intersection of healthcare transformation, AI strategy, and systems architecture. With over two decades inside hospitals and health organizations, he designs macro-to-micro architectures that connect clinical workflows, data pipelines, and AI engines into coherent, antifragile systems.

His work focuses on Symbiotic Intelligence — aligning human judgment, institutional governance, and machine intelligence to reduce failure modes, increase resilience, and make health systems crisis-ready by design. This includes digital operating models for hospitals, Health Nation OS concepts, and architectural blueprints for AI-enabled care.

This space is the public gateway into his strategic frameworks, publications, and Digital Fortress – a curated, multi-platform ecosystem spanning scholarly output, executive insights, and architecture labs.
