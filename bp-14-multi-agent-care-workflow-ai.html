Blueprint 14

Multi-Agent Care Workflow AI

A coordinated multi-agent AI architecture enabling parallel decision-making, task delegation, and workflow execution across clinical and operational systems.

Extended Architecture Overview

The Multi-Agent Care Workflow AI introduces a collaborative ecosystem where specialized AI agents — diagnostic agents, logistics agents, scheduling agents, safety agents, financial agents — work simultaneously while adhering to strict safety constraints. This architecture eliminates delays, accelerates throughput, and enables intelligent division of labor.

Agent Classes

  • Clinical Reasoning Agents
  • Scheduling & Throughput Agents
  • Patient Coordination Agents
  • Safety & Compliance Agents
  • Financial & Resource Optimization Agents

Coordination Primitives

  • Shared Context Memory
  • Task Arbitration & Priority Rules
  • Conflict Resolution Framework
  • Human-Override Governance

Dependencies

Upstream: Federated Clinical Reasoning Engine
Downstream: Unified Clinical Pipeline, Autonomous Hospital Control Loop

Questions & Notes

This blueprint is typically deployed in national orchestration engines, digital hospitals, and advanced clinical workflow automation environments.
